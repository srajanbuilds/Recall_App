Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

✨ Short version: Recall runs 100% on your device. We collect no personal data, have no servers, and your notes never leave your phone. Ever.

📋 Overview

Recall ("we", "us", "our") is a private, offline AI notes application. This Privacy Policy describes our practices for the Android app available on the Google Play Store.

Recall is built on a zero-knowledge, zero-collection architecture. We do not have a backend, user accounts, or any mechanism to access your data.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your notes, diary entries, or any text you write
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports (unless you explicitly opt in via Android's system setting)
  • Advertising identifiers

📱 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively on your device using Android's private app storage (Room/SQLite). It is never transmitted:

  • Notes: Your note titles and body text
  • Embeddings: Mathematical vector representations of your notes used for AI search (stored as binary data, not readable text)
  • Preferences: App settings such as dark mode preference and whether the AI model has been downloaded

This data is protected by Android's app sandboxing. Other apps cannot access it. When you uninstall Recall, all data is permanently deleted.

🌐 Internet Usage

Recall requires internet access for one purpose only:

  • One-time AI model download: The first time you use the "Ask Recall" feature, the app downloads the on-device AI language model (~1.5 GB) from a public model repository. This download only contains the model file — no user data is sent.

After the model is downloaded, Recall operates entirely offline. All AI inference runs locally on your device's CPU/GPU.

🤝 Third-Party Services

Recall uses the following open-source libraries. None of them collect user data:

  • ONNX Runtime (Microsoft): On-device embedding inference — MIT License
  • llama.cpp: On-device LLM inference — MIT License
  • Room (Google): Local database — Apache 2.0
  • Jetpack Compose (Google): UI framework — Apache 2.0

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, Sentry, or any analytics/crash reporting service.

👶 Children's Privacy

Recall does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app complies with COPPA and GDPR-K.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date and post the new policy at this URL. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
